1. Popular Questions

What is Ordrit? Ordrit is a grocery delivery service that delivers in as little as an hour. Ordrit connect's you with Closest Local Retailer of your choice and deliver your groceries.

What stores are supported? Ordrit is an independent business and is not necessarily affiliated with, or endorsed or sponsored by Whole Foods Market, Big Bazaar, Reliance fresh. Any Small Business in your locality can become a part of Ordrit.

Do you serve my area? We have just started our operations in Delhi & Mumbai. We're expanding slowly and steadily, so sign up with our app and let us know where you want to see Ordrit next!

2. About Ordrit

How fast do you deliver? Really fast , We deliver under an Hour

How much does delivery cost? Delivery is free, Yes Absolutely free and ALWAYS. Prices for your individual order are shown clearly at checkout.

What are your hours? We deliver from 10 am IST to 10 PM IST every day, depending on local store hours.

3. My Order

How do I check on the status of my order?You can view the status of your order by clicking the Order Status.

How do I report a problem with my order? We try to make sure that every order is perfect, If you have a problem with your order, like a missing or wrong item, please tell us! We will respond with godspeed !

How do I make a change to my order? If the store has not started processing your order ,you can add/modify or cancel your order. You can find these options on the Order Status page.
